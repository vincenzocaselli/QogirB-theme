QogirB is just a fork of UbuntuBudgie Qogir-theme (mainly focused on its Dark version), with some customizations (highlight headerbar and border of active window).

Best results with: 
* Budgie desktop (e.g. Ubuntu Budgie) and 
* OpenDesktop-Icons   -   https://www.gnome-look.org/p/1498619

Download and unzip the compressed file into ~/.themes (create .themes folder if it doesn't exists in your home); 
then apply it with GNOME Tweaks, Appearance, Applications.
